# Workshop Recording: Introduction to Stata (Dec. 3, 2021)

This page provides a video recording of an introductory workshop on Stata.

**Workshop Description:**

In this hands\-on workshop, students will learn how to read, explore, manipulate and combine data in Stata as well as run some common statistical analyses. It is suitable for new Stata users and Stata users looking to develop their familiarity with common Stata syntax.

A [software license](https://www.stata.com/order/new/edu/profplus/student-pricing/) for Stata can be purchased by University of Toronto faculty, staff, and students at a discounted rate. Please select your campus from the list of Ontario universities. Students go through the Student single\-user purchase.

All computers in the Map and Data Library, and the computer labs on the fourth and fifth floor of Robarts Library have Stata installed.
